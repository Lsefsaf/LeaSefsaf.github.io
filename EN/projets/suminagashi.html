Suminagashi
Experimentations & posters - October 2015

Discovery and experimentation of the Japanese art of marbling : the suminagashi. With the different results, the technique is used to the promotion of the book of Haruki Murakami, Kafka sur le rivage, according to the themes of metamorphosis and transition, which are very important in this book.
