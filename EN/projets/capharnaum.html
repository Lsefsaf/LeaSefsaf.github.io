Capharnaüm
Visual identity - January 2016

Wild and crazy place where coexist several activities deeply opposite : a school, a feminine catch ring, a cigar factory and Drag-Queen shows. To exist as a group, the Capharnaüm needs a global identity to be recognize. Yet, every components of the place need and want to exist by themselves. That’s the concern of the Capharnaüm which brings many opposite activities. The identity is built with a patterns range which works by superposition. In this way, each one can live apart and together.
