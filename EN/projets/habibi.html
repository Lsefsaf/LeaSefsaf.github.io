Habibi (mon amour)
Typography - January 2017

Habibi is a font inspired by my first trial of flat-feather calligraphy. Thick, voluptuous, her curves may remind oriental origins. Starting from the letter a, all of the alphabet was created respecting the narrow counter, the thick stroke, the curves. The specimen is an editorial record relating the birth (and death) of romances.
