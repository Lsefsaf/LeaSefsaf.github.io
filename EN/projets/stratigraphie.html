Préavis de désordre urbain
Posters - June 2016

Posters realized for a performance festival which takes place in the streets of Marseille : Préavis de désordre urbain

1 -The first step of the project was carried with Julie Puech.
Experimentations of silk-screen prints, based on covering effect, the bumps of walls and a patterns inspired of the city and the buildings.

2 - I’ve decided to create a serie of posters for the event. The idea was to reinvest the experimentations through photographies of the different silk-screen prints
