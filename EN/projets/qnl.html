editorial record - december 2016

Micro-events about graphism.

Internship at IPN (a collective) for a graphic design exhibition named quoi noir lignes ? about black and lines. 5 artists with different activites (painting, photography, sculpture, typography) were invited to think about this notions.

For the exhibition, Anouk Chambon and I had to think about a editorial record which combine informations about the artists, their works and the signage system.
