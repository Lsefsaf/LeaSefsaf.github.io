Blinded by the lights
Édition - Octobre 2016

At night, there’s a brand new city. Panels, graffitis disappear and let place of neons, traffic lights and all kinds of light sources. All of that contribute to create colorful atmospheres and offer to see the city in another way with new colors and shapes.

I’ve made photographies reemployed in an editorial record which offers a hazy outlook of the city. It’s like an invitation of travelling, to lose ourself through shapes and neon colors. Spaces are undefinable, everything is in motion. Néanmoins, through this pêle-mêle, there are boundries. We can found superposition of dynamic shapes which creates a visual grammar.
