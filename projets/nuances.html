Nuances de gris
Editorial - April 2016

Project realized for a workshop with Willy Muller, a graphic designer and typographer. The goal was to reinterprate one of the component of the book, to make a journal for books lovers.

My work is based on the transcription of the orality of a speech, playing with a double-reading. There is an alternating between the text we can read and the text we can « hear » meaning all of the marks of speaking like hesitations, additions etc. All of the journal is organized around the notion of grey made by the layout.
